LEGEND & HOW TO BUILD

Select: tap any line segment, or tap a station to cycle the lines through it. Tap empty ground to deselect.

Connect: drag from a station to another. Grabbing a line's ringed end extends that line.

Bend: tap anywhere on a selected line's segment to flip its curve. Splice: drag from a segment onto a bypassed station.

Detach: drag a middle stop off its line, or use the station-minus tool.

Megacities: metro lines stay within one city; intercity lines cross between cities and reach satellites.

Camera: two-finger drag to pan, pinch to zoom. Loop: connect one end to the other.
